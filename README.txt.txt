Dengue
Symptoms:

More specific:

    Skin may be red, hot, scaly, uneven, dry, swollen, or itchy, or may cause pain.
    Pain behind the eyes.
    Bleeding from the gums or nose.
    Vomiting blood.

May or may not have:

    Muscle, joint, or bone pain.
    Nausea.
    Vomiting.
    Enlarged lymph nodes.
    Severe abdominal pain.
    Persistent vomiting.
    Rapid breathing.
    Fatigue.
    Restlessness.
    Blood in stool.
    Fever over 40 degrees (headache).

Hepatitis
Symptoms:

More specific:

    Fatigue (asthenia).
    Acholia (white or light yellow stools).
    Coluria (urine color "coca cola").
    Jaundice (yellow color in mucous membranes, skin, and/or eyes).

May or may not have:

    Pain in the right hypochondrium.
    Abdominal distension.

May also have general symptoms:

    Nausea.
    Vomiting.
    Fever.
    Diarrhea.
    Headache.
    Weight loss.

Respiratory Diseases
Symptoms:

More specific:

    Cough.
    Rhinitis, runny nose (nasal secretion and congestion).

May have:

    Fever.
    Headache.
    Muscle pain (myalgia).
